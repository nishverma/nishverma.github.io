About

I study quantum geometry and correlated phenomena in moir√© and 2D materials. My current interests span time-dependent quantum geometric tensors, entanglement in topological systems, and excitonic instabilities.

Research Highlights

  • Local basis for topological bands. A framework for constructing local orbitals out of topological band wavefunctions.
  • Time dependent QGT and linear response. A unified framework connecting quantum metric, Berry curvature, to optical conductivity.
  • Quantum geometric contributions to exciton stiffness. Identifying quantum geometric boosts to interlayer exciton condensation in TMDs.
